Clinical Trial Supply


As a pioneer of Turkish Pharmaceutical Wholesalers, we are aware that it is essential for the effectiveness of clinical trials to have a seamless supply chain of Investigational Medicinal Products or fundamentally referred to as study drugs. The effectiveness of clinical trials relies heavily on the distribution of sufficient quantities of research drugs or comparator drugs to different locations where clinical trials are performed in a timely manner such that prescription drugs can be delivered to patients on schedule at the necessary dosage. The lack of supply or delay of comparator drugs in the desired quantities and quality could affect the effectiveness of clinical trials or, worse yet, could place the safety of patients at risk. With high hands-on experience in managing, storing and delivering drugs for clinical trials have provided us with invaluable skills in addressing the challenges of the supply chain clinical trial that we may face;

  • Trade laws covering the storage and shipment of clinical trial products vary across countries. As a result, full compliance with the applicable national and international legislation is necessary for the distribution of the goods.
  • Comparator drugs are also so susceptible to expiry dates that they need to be shipped literally on schedule to the testing venue.
  • The unique existence of some of the study drugs includes cold storage and distribution.
  • The supply specifications of such goods which vary in quantity over the trial duration, so it is important to be prepared to accommodate the fluctuating demands of clinical trial supplies.
  • With respect to the essential aspects of the feasibility of clinical trials and the difficulties in the supply chain of clinical trials, with its interconnected and coordinated logistics, we are the pioneer of Turkish pharmaceutical wholesalers. Moreover, we deliver solutions-oriented market skills gained through several years of pharmaceutical wholesale practice in Turkey.
