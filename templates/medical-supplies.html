Medical Supplies


Considered to be as low-cost, disposable and non-durable products and, in particular, medical supplies are important materials for the treatment of diseases and/or injuries. Therefore, in line with our core conviction that any stakeholder in national healthcare markets around the world needs to have access to high quality and inexpensive pharmaceuticals whenever needed, Mosaic Health Company's product range is as large as it includes medical supplies and equipment; consumables, various diagnostic and personalized kits, surgical, dental, laboratory and hospital equipment, and so on. As a pioneer of Turkish pharmaceutical wholesalers and with years of experience in their specific field , we provide competitively priced medical supplies and equipments, including but not limited to healthcare establishments, clinics and hospitals, on an international basis.
